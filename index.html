Caev

CAEV (Pronounced 'CAVE') is an online marketplace that allows you connect with local, safe catering services in your region.

Our primary focus is to provide users easy access to caterers

Our Story

Three fellow undergrads (Abdirahman, Noah, and Malcolm) became good friends and connected through the love of food. Outside their academic commitments, we all attended various events most of which had food or light refreshments. However, the quality of the food in most of these events was average, with some incidences that led to an unpleasant food experience. Their curiosity also led them to visit several caterers and found their food preparation locations were questionable. The team clearly knew something was missing. Frustrated with the lack of diversity, the mediocre food experiences, and lack of sufficient information about caterers led to to creation of Caev.
